### Format

The format depends on the amount of teams/people joining. In case of less than 4 teams, there is a more casual event with 5v5 customs for fun.

---

### Flex Players

Similar ranked flex players are matched together. Flex players are contacted on Sep 24, Sep 29 and Oct 3 with teams.

Alternatively, players can scout others on AG Discord #buildyourteam channel.

### Schedule

The tournament is played during two days, Friday 19.3. and Saturday 20.3.

Groups and quarter-finals are played on Friday with a 30 minute break in-between. Semi-finals and finals are played on Saturday with a 30 minute break in-between. Third-place match and final are played simultaneously.

**Friday (starts 17:00 GMT+2):**

- 16:30 Team captains ready and available on Discord
- 17:00 The first round of groups starts
- 18:15 The second round of groups starts
- 19:30 The third round of groups starts
- 20:45 A 15-minute break
- 21:00 Quarter-finals start (BO3)
- 23:30 Day ends

**Saturday (starts 13:00 GMT+2):**

- 12:30 Team captains ready and available on Discord
- 13:00 The semi-finals start (BO3)
- 16:00 A 30-min break
- 16:30 The final and the third place match start
- 19:30 Tournament ends

### Format

Three rounds with randomized groups, after which the top 8 players continue on to the finals where they play against each other twice.

**Point System:**
| Placement | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| 1st | 10 |
| 2nd | 8 |
| 3rd | 7 |
| 4th | 6 |
| 5th | 4 |
| 6th | 3 |
| 7th | 2 |
| 8th | 1 |

The final point amount is the points from the two final games plus the average from the first three games.

---

### Schedule

The first round of games starts at 18:00 or as soon as everyone is ready. The first three rounds are played immediately after each other. Before the finals there is a 20 minute break.

---

### Stream

The games aren't streamed, but all players are recommended to join AG Discord during the plays to enjoy the atmosphere and competitive spirit!

### Format

The format depends on the amount of teams/people joining. Teams can have one substitute player and swap around players between matches.

---

### Rules

- Each team has two 60 second timeouts per game, one per half
- Teams can agree together which server to use
- Mode: Standard, Allow cheats: OFF, Overtime Win By Two: ON
- Teams have 10 minutes to join the game lobby once created

---

### Map Veto

**Best of 1:**

- Team A bans map
- Team B bans map
- Team A bans map
- Team B bans map
- Team A bans map
- Team B bans map
- The only map left in the pool is picked
- Team A picks side

**Best of 3:**

- Team A bans map
- Team B bans map
- Team A picks map, Team B picks side
- Team B picks map, Team A picks side
- Team A bans map
- Team B bans map
- The only map left is picked for game 3
- Team A picks side

---

### Flex Players

There is a sign-up form for flex players. Similar ranked players are matched together. Alternatively, players can go to the Aalto Gamers discord #teambuilding channel to make a team.

### Format

**Game format:** Duos

**Group stage:** 4 teams each, top 2 move on. BO1.

**Knockout stage:** BO3 and the final is BO5. Single elimination. Bronze game is played.

### Sign-up

Players who sign up solo receive information about their to-be teammate before Saturday so there is still time to prepare synergies. Teammates are matched based on rank.

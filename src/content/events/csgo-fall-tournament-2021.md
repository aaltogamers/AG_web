### Format

Double elimination with BO1. Finals BO3.

---

### Flex Players

You can join the tournament alone or with incomplete teams but it is recommended to join as a full team. Players can find others in the "build-your-team" channels on Discord and Telegram.

---

### Challenges

Special in-game challenges are available during the tournament!

---

### Schedule

The tournament is played during two days, Friday 19.11. and Saturday 20.11.

**Friday:**

- 16:30 Team captains ready and available on Discord
- 17:00 The first round starts
- 18:00 The second round starts
- 19:00 The third round starts
- 20:00 30 minute break
- 20:30 The fourth round starts (upper bracket final)
- 21:30 The fifth round starts (lower bracket quarters)
- 22:30 The sixth round starts (lower bracket semi)

**Saturday:**

- 14:00 The lower bracket final starts
- 15:00 The grand final starts (BO3)
- 18:00 The tournament ends

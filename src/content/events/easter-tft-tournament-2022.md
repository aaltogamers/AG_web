### Format

Three rounds with randomized groups, after which the top 8 players continue on to the finals where they play against each other twice for a total of five games.

**Point System:**
| Placement | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| 1st | 8 |
| 2nd | 7 |
| 3rd | 6 |
| 4th | 5 |
| 5th | 4 |
| 6th | 3 |
| 7th | 2 |
| 8th | 1 |

The final point amount is the points from the two final games plus the average from the first three games.

**Tiebreakers in order:**

1. More first places
2. Higher average placement
3. Higher placement in the latest game
